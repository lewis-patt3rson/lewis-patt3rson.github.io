Internship Experience

MIS - NWRC

What was our task?

Myself and two other students were tasked with joining MIS for three weeks to assist them in helping a department of the North West Regional College, as MIS are the team who handle the college's software issues and development.

We were tasked to create a system to allow for electronic attendance registering for students in UCAS workshops, which would allow the careers department to understand if there was a correlation between student attendance at UCAS workshops compared to applications to universities, in an attempt to value the worth and effectiveness of the workshops.

How did we do it?

We first decided to design a database design from a requirements gathering meeting, from which we then delegated work accordingly. My group members were tasked with creating a record for safeguarding students and one for viewing ucas reports, whilst I worked on the creation of the workshop system as well as the report generating and viewing for the system, more details are shown below with screenshots.

During our third week of the placement the UK government initiated a lockdown due to the coronavirus, meaning we had to compelte the system from home with online communication with the client and MIS to ensure we met requirements for both.

Creating a workshop

Workshop admins would choose from the list of comboboxes to set which workshop they were taking, at what campus with their name as the co-ordinator. They can then add the course that is taking this workshop, or if they want they can search for the course as shwon in the course finder.

The course finder allows co-ordinators to search for classes by names filtered by what they have already typed and updated each time text in the search bar is updated, or add a course if it is not already accounted for in the system as we had limited access to the database due to COVID-19 and had to implement an Excel set to SQL.

Finally, once the workshop's details are established, the student's names and emails can be recorded to the system.

Viewing Details

Admins could also use the system to view workshops, by choosing a workshop from the list (which can be filtered). Once a workshop is selected, the students can be viewed in a new window as shown inside the list of workshops.

There is also the addition of date filters for start and end dates to search within for workshops, to add another method of filtering for the careers academy.

This data could then be exported to Excel using the button provided, allowing for reports to be made.
